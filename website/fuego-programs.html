Fuego-based Game-Playing Programs

These programs are built on top of the Fuego libraries. Please send notifications about further such programs to the fuego-devel mailing list.

For information on other programs that use Fuego for performance testing, see the publications page.

Fuego-based Go Programs

  • The "regular" Fuego is part of the distribution. Open source.
  • BlueFuego - MPI-based parallel version of Fuego, by R. Segal
  • MP-Fuego - MPI-based parallel version of Fuego, by K. Yoshizoe.
  • RLGO - Reinforcement learning and simulation-based search in computer Go. Implements D. Silver's Dyna2 architecture. Open source.

Fuego-based Programs for Other Games

  • Hex: MoHex won four computer Olympiads, in 2009, 2010, 2011 and 2013. Open source.
  • NoGo: BobNoGo (open source) and FxNoGo.
  • Konane and DisKonnect: dkf plays Konane and DisKonnect probably better than humans. Open source.
